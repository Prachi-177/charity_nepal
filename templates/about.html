About CharityNepal

Learn about our mission, our team, and our commitment to creating a better Nepal.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a transparent and effective platform for fundraising, connecting donors with impactful charity cases across Nepal. We aim to empower individuals and organizations to make a real difference in the lives of those in need.

Our Vision

We envision a Nepal where every person has the opportunity to thrive, supported by a strong community of givers. We strive to be the most trusted and accessible fundraising platform in the country.

Our Values

  • Transparency: We believe in complete openness in all our operations.
  • Impact: We are focused on creating measurable and sustainable change.
  • Community: We foster a supportive network of donors, fundraisers, and beneficiaries.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of honesty and accountability.
